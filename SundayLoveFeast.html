Sunday Love Feast

A spiritual gathering with prasadam distribution, kirtan, and discourse

View Schedule Volunteer

Experience Divine Bliss

The Sunday Love Feast is a weekly celebration where devotees come together to glorify the Supreme Lord through chanting, philosophy, and sanctified food.

Prasadam Distribution

Enjoy delicious vegetarian meals prepared with devotion and offered to Lord Krishna.

Ecstatic Kirtan

Participate in congregational chanting of the holy names with musical instruments.

Spiritual Discourse

Learn timeless wisdom from Bhagavad-gita and Srimad Bhagavatam.

Program Schedule

Every Sunday from 9:00 AM to 2:00 PM

9:00 AM - 10:00 AM

Morning Arati

Deity greeting ceremony with bhajans and prayers

10:00 AM - 11:30 AM

Bhagavad-gita Class

Practical wisdom for modern life from the sacred scripture

11:30 AM - 12:30 PM

Harinam Sankirtan

Congregational chanting of the holy names

12:30 PM - 2:00 PM

Prasadam Distribution

Sacred vegetarian feast served to all attendees
