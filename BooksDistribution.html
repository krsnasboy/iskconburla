Ways to Distribute Books

Choose a method that suits your temperament and schedule

Street Sankirtan

Distribute books in public places like markets, parks, and busy streets. This is the most dynamic and exciting form of book distribution where you can meet many people in a short time.

  • Great for extroverts
  • Immediate results
  • Team atmosphere
Join Next Outing

House-to-House

Visit residential areas and share books with families. This method allows for more personal interactions and deeper conversations about spiritual topics.

  • More personal interactions
  • Can develop relationships
  • Peaceful environment
Join Neighborhood Team

Institutional

Place books in libraries, schools, universities, and other institutions. This method reaches intellectuals and students who can deeply study the philosophy.

  • Reaches influential people
  • Books get multiple readers
  • Systematic approach
Contact Institution Team

Digital

Share e-books and audiobooks through social media, email, and other digital platforms. Perfect for those who prefer online outreach.

  • Global reach
  • Can be done from home
  • 24/7 distribution
