Discover ISKCON Burla

A spiritual oasis dedicated to spreading the teachings of Lord Krishna

Our Divine Mission

ISKCON Burla was established in 2010 with the divine purpose of cultivating spiritual growth and spreading Krishna consciousness throughout Western Odisha. Our temple serves as a beacon of peace and wisdom in the community.

Following in the footsteps of His Divine Grace A.C. Bhaktivedanta Swami Prabhupada, we strive to make the timeless wisdom of the Bhagavad-gita accessible to all.

Our Journey

2010

Foundation

ISKCON Burla was officially established with a small group of devotees conducting weekly programs in a rented space.

2013

Land Acquisition

Acquired the current temple land through the generous donations of devotees and well-wishers.

2016

Temple Construction

Completed construction of the main temple building and installed the beautiful deities of Radha Krishna.

2020

Community Expansion

Launched multiple community outreach programs including food distribution and youth initiatives.

Our Core Values

Spiritual Growth

We provide authentic spiritual knowledge and practices to help individuals progress in their Krishna consciousness.

Selfless Service

All activities are performed as an offering to Lord Krishna, without expectation of personal gain.

Cow Protection

We maintain a goshala and promote the importance of protecting and honoring mother cows.

Scriptural Study

Regular classes and study groups help devotees deeply understand Vedic wisdom.

Our Divine Personalities

Radha Krishna

The original Supreme Personality of Godhead and His eternal consort, the embodiment of divine love.

Srila Prabhupada

The founder-acharya of ISKCON who brought Krishna consciousness to the Western world.

Gaura Nitai

Lord Chaitanya Mahaprabhu and Lord Nityananda, who appeared to spread the sankirtan movement.
