Our Regular Activities

Daily spiritual programs to nourish your soul

Morning Aarti

Daily at 4:30 AM - Experience the divine awakening with Mangala Aarti followed by Japa meditation.

4:30 AM - 6:00 AM Open to all

Prasadam Distribution

Daily at 12:30 PM - Partake in sanctified vegetarian meals offered to Lord Krishna with devotion.

12:30 PM - 2:00 PM Open to all

Bhagavad-gita Class

Weekdays at 6:30 PM - Learn the timeless wisdom of the Bhagavad-gita with practical applications.

6:30 PM - 7:30 PM Open to all

Evening Kirtan

Daily at 7:00 PM - Immerse yourself in devotional chanting and experience spiritual ecstasy.

7:00 PM - 8:30 PM Open to all

Gau Seva

Weekends at 9:00 AM - Participate in serving the sacred cows at our goshala.

9:00 AM - 11:00 AM Registration required

Sunday Feast

Every Sunday at 12:00 PM - Special program with discourse, kirtan and grand feast.

10:00 AM - 2:00 PM Open to all

Special Events & Festivals

Celebrate the divine with us throughout the year

Jan 14

Makar Sankranti

Special abhishekam and kite flying festival with prasadam distribution.

Mar 10

Gaura Purnima

Celebration of Lord Chaitanya's appearance with 24-hour kirtan and feast.

Aug 15

Jhulan Yatra

Five-day swing festival for Radha-Krishna with cultural programs.

Sep 5

Radhashtami

Celebration of Srimati Radharani's appearance with special decorations.

Oct 20

Diwali

Festival of lights with Lakshmi Puja and grand fireworks display.

Dec 25

Srila Prabhupada's Disappearance

Special remembrance ceremony and reading from Prabhupada's books.
