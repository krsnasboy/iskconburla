Benefits of Becoming a Nitya Sevak

  • Fixed seva schedule for better spiritual discipline
  • Special guidance from senior devotees
  • Priority participation in temple events
  • Deeper connection with the temple community
  • Spiritual advancement through regular service

Requirements:

Minimum 6 months commitment, regular attendance, and sincere attitude of service
