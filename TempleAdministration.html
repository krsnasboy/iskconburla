Temple Policies & Guidelines

Code of Conduct

All temple residents and visitors are expected to maintain high standards of behavior. This includes dressing modestly, refraining from intoxication, and showing respect to the Deities and senior devotees.

  • No meat, fish, or eggs on temple premises
  • No intoxication of any kind
  • Proper dress code must be followed
  • Respectful behavior at all times

Donation Policy

All donations to ISKCON Burla are used exclusively for temple maintenance, preaching programs, and charitable activities. We maintain complete transparency in our financial dealings.

  • Donations can be made via cash, check, or online
  • Tax exemption certificates provided
  • Monthly donation programs available
  • Special projects can be sponsored

Volunteer Guidelines

Volunteers are the backbone of our temple. We welcome those who wish to serve Krishna with devotion and sincerity.

  • Regular attendance is appreciated
  • Training provided for all services
  • Commitment to spiritual standards required
  • Age-appropriate services available
