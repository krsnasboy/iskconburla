Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our temple and activities

The temple is open daily from 4:30 AM to 8:30 PM. Morning arati begins at 4:45 AM and evening arati at 6:30 PM. Special programs may extend these hours on festival days.

Your offering is lovingly used in Krishna’s service — from decorating the deities and feeding devotees, to preserving cows and spreading spiritual wisdom. It becomes a sacred act of devotion that benefits many souls.

Sunday Feast is open to all without registration. It starts at 5:00 PM every Sunday with kirtan, followed by lecture and prasadam distribution. Volunteers are welcome to help with preparations starting at 2:00 PM.
